Understand the Rules & GuidelinesBefore Using Our Services

Privacy Policy

Crestlane Group respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and business information with a high standard of confidentiality and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website, submit a form, communicate with our team, or engage our services through www.crestlanegroup.com. By using our website or services, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy and consent to data practices described here, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

We collect only information that is reasonably necessary for service engagement and client communication. This generally includes your name, email address, phone number, company information, and project details you provide. We do not intentionally collect sensitive or excessive personal data unrelated to our service delivery. If you choose not to provide required details, we may be unable to respond fully to your inquiry or deliver requested services.

Information collected is used solely for legitimate business purposes, including responding to inquiries, preparing proposals, managing projects, delivering services, providing support, maintaining records, and meeting legal obligations. We do not sell, rent, license, or trade your personal information to third parties. We also do not share client information for advertising by external parties or for unrelated commercial gain.

Personal Information Collection

When you submit a form, email us, book a consultation, or request a proposal, we may collect contact and project-related information necessary to evaluate your needs and execute services effectively. Depending on the engagement, this may include your name, email, phone number, company name, service requirements, project goals, timelines, brand assets, and related correspondence. We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device information, or website usage patterns for security, analytics, and performance improvement. We collect only what is necessary and proportionate to provide our services. Where payment transactions are processed through third-party payment providers, those providers handle payment details under their own terms and privacy policies, and we do not store full payment card information on our systems.

Personal Information You Provide to Us

All client projects, communications, files, credentials, strategic plans, and business information are treated as strictly confidential. We do not disclose, publish, or share client work, project files, credentials, or private business data with anyone outside Crestlane Group except where disclosure is required by law or expressly authorized by the client in writing. Access to client information is limited to team members and approved partners who need the information to deliver agreed services and are subject to confidentiality obligations.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect client data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Information is retained only for as long as needed for service delivery, support, compliance, dispute resolution, and business recordkeeping, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized where practicable. We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes, and the updated version becomes effective upon publication on this page.
